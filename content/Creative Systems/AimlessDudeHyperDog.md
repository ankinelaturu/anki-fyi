# Aimless Dude & Hyper Dog

A daily illustrated diary about two idiots exploring the world together.

Light. Cozy. Slightly chaotic. No productivity. No plans. Just life.

Each frame pairs a square illustration with a **Caiku** — a prehistoric micro-poem written in caveman-simple language.

Images are expected in `/images/adhd/` and named `d0.png`, `d1.png`, `d2.png`, ... `d125.png`.
