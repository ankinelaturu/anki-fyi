# ask-rudy.md

## What It Is

Ask Rudy is a retrieval-style Q&A experiment focused on asking questions over local or page-level knowledge.

## Technical Direction

- Local or browser-adjacent knowledge retrieval
- Question answering over grounded content
- Lightweight human-friendly interface
- Exploration of AI as a companion for understanding content

## Why It Matters

Ask Rudy maps closely to the future of AI-assisted browsing and personal knowledge tools: users should be able to ask questions about the content in front of them and receive grounded, contextual answers.
