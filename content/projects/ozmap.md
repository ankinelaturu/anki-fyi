## What It Is

OzMap is a learning exploration system that treats knowledge as a map rather than a list. Users can begin with a topic and progressively discover related concepts, dependencies, and areas worth exploring next.

## Why I Built It

Most AI interactions are linear: ask a question, get an answer. OzMap explores a different model where the system helps generate and organize the learning journey itself.

## Technical Highlights

- Graph-based topic organization
- Dynamic learning trail generation
- Branching exploration paths
- Interactive navigation and discovery
- Structured progression through related concepts

## Technical Challenges

The challenge is balancing structure and flexibility. Too much generated content becomes overwhelming; too little structure makes the map feel shallow. The system must generate useful learning paths without creating noise.

## Why It Matters

OzMap reflects my broader interest in AI-native education systems and interactive knowledge exploration. It is less about answering questions and more about helping people discover what they should learn next.
