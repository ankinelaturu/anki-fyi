# ABOUT_ANKI.md

Principal Engineer specializing in **AI-native orchestration systems, developer platforms, and runtime workflows**.

I have 20+ years of experience building scalable execution platforms and developer tooling across enterprise and embedded environments, with recent focus on AI-driven workflows that combine deterministic execution models with adaptive LLM capabilities.

## Current Focus

- AI orchestration workflows
- Agentic workflows and deterministic execution paths
- Developer platforms and interactive authoring tools
- Browser-native and local-first AI systems
- Retrieval workflows, embeddings, and function calling
- TypeScript platform architecture
- Low-latency interactive systems

## What I Build

I like building systems that turn complex runtime behavior into usable tools: visual workflow editors, AI-assisted authoring systems, local-first guardrails, retrieval-based interactions, and products where humans can iteratively collaborate with AI rather than rely on one-shot generation.

## Professional Throughline

From embedded JVM runtimes at Sun Microsystems, to mobile and web application frameworks at Oracle, to recent independent AI systems and developer tools, my work has consistently focused on execution platforms, developer productivity, and interactive systems that make complex technology usable.
