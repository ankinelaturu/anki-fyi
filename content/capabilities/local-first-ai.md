# Local-First AI

Systems where inference, retrieval, and guardrails run on-device or in the browser — minimizing data exposure while preserving useful AI assistance.

## Themes

- Browser extensions and in-tab inspection
- Embeddings and retrieval without cloud dependency
- Governance-friendly AI workflows for enterprises
