# Lessons from Six in Twelve

Reflections on building multiple AI and developer-tool experiments in a short cycle — what scaled, what did not, and what to keep.

## Takeaways

- Favor composable primitives over monolithic demos
- Local-first constraints sharpen product thinking
- Explorer-style knowledge bases accelerate iteration
